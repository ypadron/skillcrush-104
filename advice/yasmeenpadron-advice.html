Hi my name is Yasmeen and I have some advice for you!

Bust out those Cheatsheets

Memorize the common Terminal & GIT commands as soon as you possibly can! Trust me when I tell you that it will make EVERYTHING easier!

Join a local Ruby meetup group

If there's a local Ruby meetup you can join I encourage you to do it! I find the help that I receive from meetups invaluable and making new friends is always fun.

In addition to local meetups, you should definitely look for Ruby workshops. I've been to two Ruby workshops for women in the past nine months and I highly recommend them.

Keep calm and carry on

Try not to get too frustrated when you encounter problems. For me, one moment everything makes sense and I'm making great progress and then a few minutes later I'll encounter a problem that sets me back for hours.

Coding can be frustrating. When you get frustrated you should take a break.

Finally, other people have probably mentioned this but it's worth repeating - code a little everyday rather than a lot at one time. I find it's easier to retain information in small bits rather than large chunks. Good luck!!
